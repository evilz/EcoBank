# Home Screen - Official EcoBank Design Applied

## ✅ Design Updates Applied

### 1. Hero Balance Card
- **Before**: Blue background (#1A56DB)
- **After**: Green official (#7ED957)
- **Radius**: RadiusCard (24px)
- **Shadow**: Official card shadow

### 2. Quick Actions
- **Before**: 3-column buttons
- **After**: 4-column icon + label (Envoyer, Reçu, Virement, Plus)
- **Style**: Light green background (#F0F9E8) with icons
- **Radius**: Pill shape (28px)

### 3. Evolution Chart Section
- **New**: Added balance evolution card
- **Badge**: +12.5% increase badge with light green background
- **Typography**: Official sizes and weights

### 4. Accounts Summary
- **New**: List of all accounts
- **Card Style**: White cards with official shadow
- **Balance**: Green text (#7ED957) to highlight
- **Details**: IBAN and balance display

### 5. Recent Operations
- **Before**: Simple list with borders
- **After**: Cards with avatar area, label, date, and amount
- **Avatar**: Light green background icon (💳)
- **Layout**: 3-column grid with proper spacing
- **Amount**: Color-coded (green for debit, red for credit)

### 6. Global
- **Background**: #F7F8F5 (official light background)
- **Cards**: #FFFFFF (white)
- **Text**: Official colors (#1B1D1F, #7A7F85)
- **Accent**: #C6FF00 (yellow) for highlights
- **Spacing**: Official spacing scale (4, 8, 16, 24, 32)
